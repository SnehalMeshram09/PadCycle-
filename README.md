# ♻🌟 PadCycle – Recycle. Reward. Reform.

**PadCycle** is a digital platform that encourages sustainable menstrual waste management by enabling users to return used non-biodegradable sanitary pads (safely packaged). Users track their contributions, earn rewards, and watch their waste transform into eco-friendly materials such as insulation boards, compost bricks, and road bases.

---

## 🎯 Project Goal

Build a user-friendly platform that:
- Tracks returned used sanitary pads
- Rewards users for safe disposal
- Partners with recycling facilities to repurpose waste
- Drives behavioral change through gamification and AI

---

## 🔧 System Overview

### 🧩 1. Mobile/Web App Interface – For Users
- 📲 Register / Login
- 📝 Log pad usage and schedule pickup/drop
- 📷 Upload packaging proof (image/QR code)
- 📊 Track number of pads returned
- 🔁 See how their pads were transformed
- ⭐ Earn Green Stars
- 🎁 Redeem stars for:
  - Discounts on sustainable products
  - Coupons
  - Donating eco-products to underserved communities

### 🚛 2. Collection & Processing Backend – For NGOs/Admin
- 📦 Assign pickups or designate drop-off zones
- ✅ Validate returns via manual/AI image verification
- ⚖ Track weight/volume of pads
- 🔄 Forward waste to recycling partners
- 📈 Live dashboard:
  - Total pads collected
  - Environmental impact
  - Top contributors

### 🎁 3. Rewards + Loyalty Engine
- 1 pad = 1 Green Star ⭐  
- Bonus stars for:
  - 🗣 Referrals
  - ♻️ Switching to sustainable menstrual products
  - 🔰 Tier achievements (Bronze → Eco-Champion)
  - 📲 Social sharing of impact

### 🧠 4. AI + Gamification Modules
- 🔍 Image AI for safe packaging verification
- 🧭 AI-predicted logistics optimization
- 🧱 AR View: What your pad became (brick, tile, etc.)
- 🏆 Leaderboards and milestone badges

---

Partner with recycling orgs/startups to turn pads into:
